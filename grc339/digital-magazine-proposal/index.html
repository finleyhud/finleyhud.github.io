Introduction

White & Black

White and Black (may change name) is a digital magazine aimed towards anyone with an interest in black and white photography. The publication features artist spotlights, process breakdowns, historical essays, and curated showcases that celebrate the timelessness of black and white aesthetics in both analog and digital contexts.
